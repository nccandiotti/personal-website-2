About Me:

Hi! My name is Nicole Candiotti, and I am a speech-language pathologist, currently located in Central New York with the goal of returning to NYC by the end of 2022. In the past five years, I've embarked on a variety of educational pursuits, ranging from studying comparative literature in Paris, France; communication sciendes and disorders in NYC; a clinical fellowship in speech-language pathology at a level 1 trauma center in Rochester, New York; and finally, here: studying web development and software engineering from my living room in Syracuse, New York by way of the Flatiron School. My professional experiences to date have primarily focused on assessment and treatment of adults with speech, language, cognitive, and swallowing deficits secondary to stroke, traumatic brain injury, progressive neurological diseases, old age, post surgical complications, cancer, and trauma in acute care and acute inpatient rehab. My long term goal is to collaborate with others to create apps that improve the lives of individuals with brain injuries, their caretakers, and their medical providers. Short term goal: have fun and figure out what the heck this software engineering business is all about!
